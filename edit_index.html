Slow Cooker

Salsa Chicken

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breast (6 ounce each)
  • 1 Jar of salsa (16 ounce)
  • 1 3/4 cups frozen corn, thawed
  • 1 can
    • pinto beans, rinsed and drained (15 ounce)
  • 1 can
    • no salt added black beans, rinsed and drained (15 ounce)
  • 1 can
    • diced tomatoes and green chiles, undrained (10 ounce)
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper

    • Optional: hot cooked rice, cubed avocado, chopped fresh tomato, sliced green onions and lime wedges

Kitchen Equipment

  • Slow Cooker
  • Rice Cooker
  • Knife
  • Can opener
  • Cutting board
  • Measuring Cups

Directions

Place chicken in a 4- or 5-qt. slow cooker. Top with salsa, corn, beans, diced tomatoes and chiles, sugar, salt and pepper. Cook, covered, on low until a thermometer inserted in chicken reads 165Â°, 3-4 hours. If desired, serve with optional ingredients.
